My Involvement on Campus

Vice President of Marketing - Design Interactive

As the VP of Marketing for Design Interactive, I lead our initiatives at the intersection of design and technology. In this role, I create compelling marketing materials and manage our social media platforms, including LinkedIn and Instagram. My responsibilities include producing graphic design content and executing targeted campaigns to promote our activities and engage our audience.

Marketing and DEI Representative - Wisconsin Alumni Student Board

In my role with the Wisconsin Alumni Student Board (WASB), I serve as a Marketing and Diversity, Equity, and Inclusion (DEI) Representative. I drive marketing initiatives for WASB events by crafting engaging digital materials that enhance outreach and foster school spirit among students, alumni, and the broader community.

Undergraduate Research Assistant - Schloss Visual Reasoning Lab

Additionally, I am an Undergraduate Research Assistant at the Schloss Visual Reasoning Lab within the Psychology Department. Here, I conduct and oversee participant engagement in visual reasoning experiments, ensuring compliance with research protocols and ethical guidelines. I contribute to weekly research meetings, present findings related to virtual reality and color associations, and collaborate with my peers to shape the direction of our research.

Through these roles, I am committed to enhancing my skills while contributing to meaningful projects that benefit both the university community and my professional growth.
